(04) Operating principle

Radical
transparency.

We share every rate card, every milestone, and every unknown in writing before a contract is signed. Partners see the same Figma boards, the same Notion docs and the same roadmaps we do — no performative theatre, no hidden retainers.

It is not a marketing stance. It is the only way a 14-person studio can stand next to agencies ten times our size and still win the brief on craft alone.

— ELIAS TORRES FOUNDING PARTNER
(05) About the studio

A studio built
for the long game.

Journey is fourteen designers, engineers and strategists working out of London, New York and Seoul. We only take on four partnerships a year, and we protect that number the same way we protect our weekends.

Our team has shipped work for Monzo, Patagonia, Toyota and roughly forty founders whose companies you will know about in three years. We are opinionated about typography, suspicious of trends, and allergic to the word "synergy".

Every engagement is led by Sarah Chen (strategy), Marcus Rivera (design) or Aisha Patel (engineering). No junior handoffs, no outsourced pods. You get the person whose name is on the contract.
